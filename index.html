About Me

I have worked in multiple facets of the real estate industry for over 25 years, including property management, commercial real estate, brokerage, and property tax consulting. After earning my Master's degree in Real Estate in 2004, I began teaching part-time in the real estate program at Tarrant County College. In 2018, I accepted a full-time position as Professor of Real Estate at Collin College.

I am a Texas Real Estate Broker and Property Tax Consultant. In the state of Washington, I am a Real Estate Managing Broker and a licensed instructor. I hold the Certified Real Estate Instructor (CREI) designation from the Texas Real Estate Teachers Association, and the Certified Distance Education Instructor (CDEI) designation.

I have served on the Professional Standards Committee for Texas Realtors since 2022, and I am the director of the CREI program at the Texas Real Estate Teachers Association. You can find out more about me on my LinkedIn profile.
